About Us

Our Story

CheesyBites has been serving authentic Italian pizza since 1985. What started as a small family business has grown into a beloved local institution, but we've never forgotten our roots.

We use only the finest ingredients imported directly from Italy, including San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and extra virgin olive oil. Our dough is made fresh daily and aged for 48 hours to develop the perfect flavor and texture.
